ABOUT BV

Banasthali Vidyapith is a prominent women's university located in Tonk, Rajasthan, India. It was founded in 1935 by Smt. Ratan Shastri and Pandit Hiralal Shastri. Banasthali Vidyapith is known for its focus on women's education and offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in various fields. The university is recognized by the University Grants Commission (UGC) and is known for its emphasis on holistic education, including academic, cultural, and physical development. Banasthali Vidyapith has a unique residential campus and is committed to promoting the overall well-being of its students.
