Bachelor of Architecture program at Banasthali Vidyapith approved by the Council of Architecture, New Delhi

The School of Architecture& Planning is a new venture at BanasthaliVidyapith offering a full-time 5-year degree program. The inception of the school was done to provide the students of architecture with much-needed quality education with the help of modern infrastructure, tools, and equipment so that the students can produce more innovative & sustainable design solutions to fulfill societal and professional obligations. The students will be trained for hands-on experience in architecturalfield.

    Banasthali Vidyapith has been conferred the prestigious Rajiv Gandhi National Sadbhavana Award. The award has been received by Prof. Siddharth Shastri and Prof. Ina Shastri from former Vice President Hamid Ansari ji and Smt. Sonia Gandhi ji in an award ceremony at Jawahar Bhawan Auditorium, New Delhi. The award, instituted in 1992 to mark the golden jubilee of the Quit India Movement, is given to an individual or institution for a special contribution towards peace, communal harmony, and national unity. This award is a recognition of the hard work and dedication of decades worth of collective efforts by the founders, caretakers, students, teachers, and members of the Banasthali family.
